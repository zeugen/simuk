International Foundation Program

The International Foundation Programme is of one academic year duration and prepares students for our undergraduate programmes. It provides a bridge for those who have successfully completed 12 years schooling with a good school leaving qualification, but do not possess the qualifications for direct entry to one of our degree courses.  This programme would also be extremely valuable for those who took ‘A’ levels or equivalent, but whose grades may have fallen below the expectations to qualify for an undergraduate programme.

Undergraduate Programs

The undergraduate programmes we offer at Edulink International College are delivered around a range of core subjects which provide a solid education in the essential areas of business. Go to the separate program pages to learn more about the programs, entry requirements as well as the course outline/overview.

Professional Programmes

These are programs that are designed to make students industry – ready professionals in different areas. Currently, we are offering Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering(ASDE), Certificate in Multimedia (CIM), Arena Multimedia Specialist Programme (AMSP), Microsoft Office Specialist 2013, Certificate of Proficiency in information System Management (CPISM)
